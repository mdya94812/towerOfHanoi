Towers of Hanoi

 

Theory

The Tower of Hanoi is a puzzle where you have three rods and a number of disks of different sizes. The goal is to move all the disks from the source rod to the destination rod, following these rules:


  • Only one disk can be moved at a time.
  • A disk can only be placed on a larger disk or on an empty rod.
  • You can use the auxiliary rod as a helper.
Solution
  • If there is 1 disk, simply move it to the destination.
  • For more than 1 disk:
    1. Move n-1 disks from Source to Auxiliary.
    2. Move the nth disk from Source to Destination.
    3. Move n-1 disks from Auxiliary to Destination.
